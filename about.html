About Us

About Garad

Garad Foundation is a registered non-governmental organization based in Thane. We believe in the boundless potential of youth to shape a brighter future. Founded with a vision to empower, educate, and elevate, we are committed to making, a meaningful impact on society, with a special focus on children and education

What We do

EMPOWERMENT

We empower youth in our Ngo through social work. Our leadership development initiatives activities instill confidence, leadership skills and sense of social responsibility.

EDUCATION EXCELLENCE

Through innovative programs. Educational kits we enhance learning environment and promote literacy.

COMMUNITY ENGAGEMENT

We believe in power of communities. We work hand in hand with local communities to create sustainable solutions.

Why Choose GARAD Foundation

Passionate Team

Our dedicated team of Volunteers, professionals and partners share a common Passion for making a difference, driving our commitment to excellence.

Transparency

We operate with utmost transparency and accountability, ensuring that every donation and resources is used effectively to benefit those in need.

Need base assessment work

We working on need base assessment i.e. what is need of individual and community. It will help every single individual to actually fulfil there need.
