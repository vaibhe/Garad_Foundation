Selfie with Bappa: Celebrating Ganeshotsav with a Green Heart

A Fun and Eco-Friendly Way to Spread Environmental Awareness

Introduction

In 2019, we launched an exciting and innovative campaign called Selfie with Bappa. This campaign was all about combining the power of social media with the love for Ganeshotsav to promote environmental awareness among the youth. Our goal was to encourage everyone to celebrate this beloved festival in a way that’s kind to our planet.

Purpose of the Campaign

The main purpose of the Selfie with Bappa campaign was to spread the message of celebrating Ganeshotsav in an eco-friendly manner. We wanted to highlight the importance of reducing pollution during the festival, especially by encouraging the use of eco-friendly Ganpati idols made from natural materials. Through this campaign, we aimed to inspire people to think about how their actions impact the environment and to make choices that help protect it.

Activities and Highlights

The campaign invited participants to take selfies with their Ganpati idols and share them on social media platforms using the hashtag #SelfieWithBappa. It was a fun and interactive way to engage with the community and spread an important message.

As the selfies started rolling in, social media was flooded with beautiful images of Ganpati idols, each accompanied by a message about the importance of celebrating the festival in an eco-friendly way. Whether it was a small clay idol or a larger one made from natural materials, each post carried the spirit of devotion and the commitment to protecting our environment.

The campaign also sparked conversations about the impact of traditional Ganesh idols made from plaster of Paris, which can cause significant environmental damage. By promoting the use of idols made from clay or other biodegradable materials, Selfie with Bappa encouraged people to make greener choices and celebrate the festival in a way that honors both tradition and nature.

Impact and Reflection

Selfie with Bappa was more than just a social media trend; it was a movement that brought together the love for Ganeshotsav and the commitment to protecting our planet. The campaign successfully raised awareness about the environmental impact of our choices and encouraged thousands of people to celebrate the festival in a more sustainable way.

The overwhelming response and the beautiful eco-friendly Ganpati idols shared online showed us that small actions can lead to big changes. By making eco-friendly choices, we can all contribute to a healthier planet while still enjoying the festivals we love.

Join Us in Celebrating Eco-Friendly Festivals

Selfie with Bappa was just the beginning of our efforts to promote environmental awareness through fun and engaging campaigns. We encourage everyone to continue celebrating festivals in a way that’s both joyful and kind to the environment. Let’s keep the spirit of Bappa alive while protecting the world we live in!
