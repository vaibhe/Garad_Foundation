Utsavachi Shidori: Spreading the Joy of Diwali through Food

A Festive Food Donation Drive to Brighten Lives

Introduction

From October 25 to 31, 2019, we organized a special event called Utsavachi Shidori—a food donation drive aimed at bringing the joy of Diwali to those who need it most. Diwali is a time of celebration, light, and delicious food, but for many underprivileged families, the festive season can be a reminder of what they lack. Our goal was to ensure that everyone, regardless of their circumstances, could enjoy the special foods and snacks that make Diwali so memorable.

Purpose of the Event

The purpose of Utsavachi Shidori was simple: to share the joy of Diwali with those who are less fortunate. We wanted to make sure that even the most disadvantaged members of our community could feel included in the festivities by enjoying the traditional foods that are such an important part of the celebration.

Activities and Highlights

During this week-long drive, our foundation collected donations of various food items, including snacks, namkeen, and sweets. The response from our community was overwhelming, with many generous contributions pouring in.

With these donations, we prepared a total of 175 food packets, each filled with a variety of delicious snacks. Our volunteers then distributed these food packets to homeless individuals and families in need, bringing a little bit of Diwali joy to those who might otherwise go without.

The happiness on the faces of those who received the food packets was priceless. For many, it wasn’t just about the food—it was about feeling remembered and cared for during a time when the rest of the world is celebrating. The sense of inclusion and warmth that Utsavachi Shidori brought to these individuals was the true highlight of the event.

Impact and Reflection

Utsavachi Shidori was a beautiful reminder of the power of giving, especially during a time of celebration like Diwali. By sharing food, we were able to share joy, hope, and a sense of belonging with those who often feel left out.

This initiative not only helped fill stomachs but also hearts, as it brought a festive spirit to those who needed it most. The success of this food drive showed us that when we come together as a community, we can make a significant difference in the lives of others.

Join Us in Spreading Joy

Utsavachi Shidori was just one way to give back during the festive season. We encourage everyone to continue finding ways to share joy and support those in need, not just during Diwali but throughout the year. Together, we can make every festival a little brighter for everyone.
